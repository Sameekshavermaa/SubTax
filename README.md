## 🧠 The Idea  

Subscriptions feel small.  
₹199 here. ₹499 there.  

But stacked together… they quietly become a **monthly financial drain**.

**SubTax** was designed to make that invisible cost impossible to ignore — by turning scattered subscriptions into a clear, emotional, and visual experience.

---

## ⚡ Features  

- 📊 **Real-time expense tracking**  
- 💸 Monthly, yearly & daily breakdown  
- 🎬 **Netflix-inspired UI with category grouping**  
- 💱 Currency switcher (INR, USD, EUR, GBP)  
- ➕ Add / ✏️ Edit / ❌ Delete subscriptions  
- 🧩 Custom categories  
- 🔒 100% local storage (no login, no backend)  

---

## 💡 Core Insight  

> **“You’re spending ₹X/day… just to exist online.”**

Instead of overwhelming users with data, SubTax focuses on a simple psychological trigger —  
turning abstract spending into something *real and unavoidable*.

---

## 🎨 Design Philosophy  

SubTax is not a finance app.  
It’s a **lifestyle product**.

### Inspired by:
- 🎬 Netflix → content layout & interaction  
- 🍏 Apple → minimalism & spacing  
- 🌿 Nature → calm, focused visual tone  

### Design Goals:
- Reduce clutter  
- Highlight what matters  
- Create emotional awareness  

---

## 🧩 How It Works  

- All subscriptions are normalized into **monthly cost**
- Automatically converted into:
  - 📅 Yearly projection  
  - ☕ Daily expense  
- Currency conversion updates everything instantly
